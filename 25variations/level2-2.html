As if

   plume

       solitary distraught

  save that encounters or skims it a midnight cap and

                   immobilizes

                 in velvet crumpled
                                 by a guffaw somber that
   rigid whiteness derisory

           in opposition

                       the
         to                   sky
                                    too much
                                  for not

                           marking exiguously

                  whosoever bitter prince

                                  of the reef puts it on
                                            like the heroic irresistible

                                       but contained
                                                  by his little reason virile in lightning
                                concerned expiatory
        and pubescent mute laughter
